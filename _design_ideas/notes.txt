Sensei Iha is the senior most practitioner of Okinawa Shorin-ryu residing in the United States 
and is ranked a Hanshi 10th Dan by the Okinawa Shorin-ryu Karate-do Association of Naha, Okinawa. 
He teaches Chibana-style shorin-ryu and refers to his method as Okinawa Shorin-ryu Shidokan Karatedo. 

On May 19th 2020, the Okinawa Prefecture Government designated him as 
Intangible Cultural Asset Holder in the category of Okinawa Karate and Kobujutsu
