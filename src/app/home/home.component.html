Truly Authentic

You've stumbeld across real Okinawan karate.

Since the 1980s we've been dedicated students of Seikichi Iha (伊波清吉), Hanshi 10th Dan, and highest ranking Shōrin-ryū master in the US.

In 2024 we were accepted directly under Morinobu Maeshiro (真栄城 守信), Hanshi 10th Dan and President of the world Shorinryu Karatedo Association in Okinawa.

The Okinawa Prefecture Government has designated both Iha and Maeshiro as Intangible Cultural Asset Holders in the Field of Okinawan Karate, a recognition of authenticity and historical preservation granted to only 9 masters alive today.

Unmatched Experience

Our instructors collectively have over 130 yrs experience. Advanced degree tests are held in Okinawa and certified by the senior masters to ensure integrity of the style.

Effective Technique

Okinawan karate was born from a need to defend against life-threatening danger. A mature attitude is required to safely practice techniques, many of which are illegal in conventional sport styles.

No movie theatrics. No wasted motion.

You will learn how to comfortably block full force attacks without pads, while developing the control to avoid hurting lower ranked students.
