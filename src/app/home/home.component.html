Authentic and Unique

Since the 1980's we have had the honor to be students and friends of Sensei Iha Seikichi, Hanshi 10th Dan (link) and . highest ranking Shorin Ryu master in the United States. In 2024 we began direct affiliation with Morinobu Maeshiro ( 真栄城 守信 ), Hanshi 10th Dan and President of the Okinawa Shorinryu karatedo Association. On May 19th 2020, the Okinawa Prefecture Government designated both masters as an "Intangible Cultural Asset Holders in the Field of Okinawa Karate and and Kobujutsu", a designation granted to only 20 karateka ever.
