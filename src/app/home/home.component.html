Authentic Lineage

Since the 1980s we've been dedicated students and friends of Iha Seikichi, Hanshi 10th Dan and highest ranking Shōrin-ryū master in the United States. In 2024 we were honored with direct affiliation under Morinobu Maeshiro ( 真栄城 守信 ), Hanshi 10th Dan and President of the Okinawa Shorinryu karatedo Association. In 2020, the Okinawa Prefecture Government designated both masters as "Intangible Cultural Asset Holders in the Field of Okinawa Karate and and Kobujutsu", a designation granted to only 20 karateka ever.

Unmatched Experience

Our 4 main instructors collectively have over 130 yrs experience. Instructor and advanced degree tests are held in Okinawa and certified by the senior masters to ensure integrity of the style.

Effective Technique

Okinawan karate was born out of a necessity to defend against life-threatening danger. A mature attitude is required to safely practice techniques which are illegal in conventional sport karate styles.

No flying movie theatrics. No wasted motion. No pads.
You will learn how to comfortably block full force attacks without pads, while developing the control to avoid hurting your classmates. Most importantly, you will not have to wonder “would this really work?”
