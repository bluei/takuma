Authentic Lineage

Since the 1980s we've been dedicated students and friends of Iha Seikichi, Hanshi 10th Dan, and highest ranking Shōrin-ryū master in the United States.

In 2024 we were hoored to be accepted under Morinobu Maeshiro ( 真栄城 守信 ), Hanshi 10th Dan and President of the world Shorinryu karatedo Association based in Naha, Okinawa.

In May 2020, the Okinawa Prefecture Government designated each of these masters as an "Intangible Cultural Asset Holder in the Field of Okinawan Karate", a designation granted to only 20 karateka ever. [link to article]

Unmatched Experience

Our 4 lead instructors collectively have over 130 yrs experience. Advanced degree tests are held in Okinawa and certified by the senior masters to ensure integrity of the style.

Effective Technique

Okinawan karate was born from a need to defend against life-threatening danger. A mature attitude is required to safely practice techniques, many of which are illegal in conventional sport styles.

No flying movie theatrics. No wasted motion. No pads.

You will learn how to comfortably block full force attacks without pads, while developing the control to avoid hurting lower ranked students. Most importantly, you will not have to wonder “would this really work?”
