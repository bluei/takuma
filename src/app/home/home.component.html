Authentic Lineage

Since the 1980s we've been dedicated students and friends of Seikichi Iha (伊波清吉), Hanshi 10th Dan, and highest ranking Shōrin-ryū master in the United States.

In 2024 we were accepted directly under Morinobu Maeshiro (真栄城 守信), Hanshi 10th Dan and President of the world Shorinryu karatedo Association based in Naha, Okinawa.

On May 19 2020, both Iha and Maeshiro received the designation of "Intangible Cultural Asset Holder in the Field of Okinawan Karate" by the Okinawa Prefecture Government. This recognition of authenticity and historical importance has only been granted to 20 karateka ever.

Unmatched Experience

Our 4 lead instructors collectively have over 130 yrs experience. Advanced degree tests are held in Okinawa and certified by the senior masters to ensure integrity of the style.

Effective Technique

Okinawan karate was born from a need to defend against life-threatening danger. A mature attitude is required to safely practice techniques, many of which are illegal in conventional sport styles.

No flying movie theatrics. No wasted motion.

You will learn how to comfortably block full force attacks without pads, while developing the control to avoid hurting lower ranked students.
